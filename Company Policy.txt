# 1. Welcome Message

Welcome to NovaSphere Technologies Pvt. Ltd. We are committed to building an innovative, inclusive, ethical, and growth-oriented workplace where every employee can thrive professionally and personally.

This handbook outlines the standards, expectations, responsibilities, and benefits associated with employment at NovaSphere Technologies. Every employee is expected to read, understand, and comply with the policies described in this document.

The company reserves the right to revise, amend, or discontinue any policy at its discretion.

---

# 2. Company Overview

NovaSphere Technologies Pvt. Ltd. is a global technology and consulting organization specializing in:

* Artificial Intelligence
* Cloud Computing
* Enterprise Software
* Data Analytics
* Cybersecurity Solutions
* Digital Transformation Services

Founded in 2014, the company operates across multiple regions with a commitment to innovation, customer success, operational excellence, and ethical business conduct.

# 4. Equal Employment Opportunity Policy

NovaSphere Technologies is an equal opportunity employer. Employment decisions are based on merit, qualifications, performance, and business requirements.

The company prohibits discrimination based on:

* Race
* Religion
* Gender
* Sexual orientation
* Age
* Disability
* Marital status
* National origin
* Veteran status
* Genetic information
* Political affiliation where protected by law

All employees are entitled to a respectful and professional workplace.

# 5. Code of Conduct

Employees are expected to maintain the highest standards of professional behavior.

## Employees Must:

* Treat colleagues respectfully
* Follow company policies
* Protect confidential information
* Avoid unethical behavior
* Comply with applicable laws
* Maintain professionalism with clients and vendors

## Employees Must Not:

* Engage in fraud or corruption
* Use abusive language
* Misuse company resources
* Share confidential data without authorization
* Participate in workplace harassment
* Accept bribes or kickbacks

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to termination.

# 6. Workplace Ethics

Ethical behavior is mandatory across all company operations.

Employees must:

* Report unethical practices
* Ensure accurate documentation
* Maintain honesty in reporting
* Comply with regulatory obligations

Managers are expected to lead by example and create an ethical workplace culture.

# 7. Anti-Harassment & Anti-Discrimination Policy

The company maintains zero tolerance toward workplace harassment.

## Prohibited Conduct Includes:

* Verbal abuse
* Offensive jokes
* Bullying
* Threats
* Sexual harassment
* Intimidation
* Offensive visual materials
* Unwanted physical contact

Complaints may be reported to:

* Human Resources
* Ethics Committee
* Reporting Manager
* Anonymous Hotline

Retaliation against complainants is strictly prohibited.
